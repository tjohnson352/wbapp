Schedule Guidelines

  1. General/Duty Activities: Non-teaching activities such as hallway monitoring, staff meetings, and recess duties. These are included in frametime but not counted as teaching time.
  2. Gap Violations: Allow a 5-minute buffer before and after teaching for preparation and wrap-up.
  3. Frametime Violations: Ensure a 5-minute buffer before the first lesson and after the last lesson of the day. A 10-minute buffer applies to certain subjects like art, woodwork, home economics, physical education (excluding theory), and science labs.
  4. Planning Time: Unscheduled blocks of 30 minutes or more used for lesson preparation, grading, and administrative tasks.
